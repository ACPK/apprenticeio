  • Project Spotlight

    Umbrella Today

    The Umbrella Today application is the simplest weather report a person could possibly want. We were frustrated with the constant information overload provided by web and television-based weather reporting services, so we attempted to build a service that answered the one question anyone really cares about - "do I need to bring an umbrella?" The Umbrella Today application was conceived, designed, developed and continues to be maintained entirely by thoughtbot.

    Shortbord

    Shortbord is probably the best way to advertise on the web. Social media users ("surfers") sign endorsement deals with companies ("sponsors") to display the company's logo next to the surfer's avatar on social media sites ("partners").

    thoughtbot worked with the three founders of Shortbord to design and build a Rails app from scratch. As part of our Kickstart program, the founders work out of our Boston office and we help them find and train technical hires.
