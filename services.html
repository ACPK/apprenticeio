Services

Our core business is providing web application development and design services. We use an iterative development process which will lead to a successful launch for your web application. We are involved every step of the way from initial project conception and planning through final testing and public launch.

We also provide Ruby on Rails software development training — and offer a web project “Kickstart” service.

Web Application Development and Design

We’ve been building Ruby on Rails powered web applications since before Rails 1.0 was released. Our team has the expertise and experience to take your concept and quickly turn it into a running web application.

Using an iterative development methodology, we will work with you through the design, development and launch phases of your project. Our process ensures maximum product quality will allowing for continuous flexibility and scope change all the way through launch.

Web Business Kick Start

We will work with you as you start a new business or build a new web based product. We can evaluate an existing codebase before development begins, or work from scratch side by side with your growing team. We'll work with you to make technology decisions as your business grows, and assist as you hire your own full time team.

Ruby on Rails Training

For teams just getting started on Ruby or adding Ruby as a skill-set to an existing development team, we can provide on-site training, or you can attend one of our hosted workshops.

Developers with leave with exposure to current Ruby on Rails standards, as well as our opinions on best practice for building real production-ready web applications.
