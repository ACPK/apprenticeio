We make web apps for startups
and make developers more productive.

In our consulting practice, we design and develop web apps for our clients, who are usually startups.
That work has led us to create SaaS web apps for developers and web design and development training courses.

Web Design & Development

We are a full-service, never-subcontract team of 13 web developers and 5 web designers. We have focused only on Ruby on Rails web applications since before Rails 1.0 was released in 2005.

Our process is to design the user interface first, then implement user stories. We work in weekly iterations and deploy to production often. We believe our process maximumizes product quality and flexibility of scope right up until launch.

Kick Start

We provide an extra service called "Kick Start" for startups who want it. We interview and make recommendations on candidates for the company's first full-time designers or developers (often the CTO). This is often a challenge for non-technical founders and we're happy to help. We know many people in the community and have interviewed countless people over the years.

Hoptoad

Hoptoad — the original “app error app” — is a software as service (SaaS) web application we wrote to solve a common problem we had while developing apps for clients. The service collects errors generated by web apps, and aggregates the results. It has helped thousands of developers at companies like Kickstarter, Gowalla, and Localytics fix millions of errors.
