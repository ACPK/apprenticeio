thoughtbot sponsored events

thoughtbot provides everything that you could need, including drinks and dinner, wireless, monitors, comfortable chairs and sometimes beer. Most sponsored events are located at thoughtbot's offices found on 41 Winter St, Boston Ma 02108 on the 3rd floor. See a map.

  • Boston Ruby Hackfest

    When: Second Tuesday every month

    Where: thoughtbot offices

    Ruby hackfests are a chance for Ruby, Javascript, or any-other-technology developer in Boston to get together. We patch and release open source libraries, and help each other through tricky bugs. We write code so bring a laptop and enthusiasm.

  • Boston Android Hackfests

    When: Every other Monday

    Where: thoughtbot offices, SCVGR offices

    Developers and designers do a variety of things ranging from installing Eclipse and the Android SDK to writing a widget to exploring alternative SDKs to upgrading their phone—not to mention the networking and socializing.

  • Design with Boston

    When: Every first thursday of every month

    Where: thoughtbot offices

    A local web design community. We focus on growing our experience and knowledge together, helping web designers succeed and break into the industry, and provide web designers a platform to share their ideas.
