Hackfests

Where:
thoughtbot office 41 Winter Street, 3rd floor Boston, MA Map
When:

Boston Ruby: second Tuesday every month. Calendar
Boston Android: every other Monday. Calendar

What:

The hackfests are a chance for Ruby, Android, Javascript, or any-other-technology developer in Boston to get together. We patch and release open source libraries, and help each other through tricky bugs. thoughtbot provides dinner, wireless, monitors, and comfortable chairs. We write code so bring a laptop and enthusiasm. See you at the next one!
