Concept to launch in four weeks.

Pozit is a simple tool that enables you to create invite-only discussions about any topic or question. You can invite friends to your discussion via email, Facebook and Twitter, and point them to a unique Pozit space and URL where they can post responses. Your invitees see the whole conversation, so everyone benefits from the group’s collective wisdom or foolishness. If an invitee to your Pozit knows someone who might further the conversation, she can invite him or her to join. And the discussion spaces live forever, so they can be referenced, re-kindled, and linked-to in the future. That’s it.
